Privacy Policy

Effective June 1, 2026 Last updated June 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how BlackRain Automations LLC ("BlackRain Automations," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit blackrainautomations.com (the "Site") or engage our services.

BlackRain Automations LLC is a limited liability company organized under the laws of Connecticut, United States. Our registered contact is ryan@blackrainautomations.com.

1INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect the following categories of information:

(a) Information you provide directly. When you book a Blueprint or contact us, we collect your name, email address, business name, phone number (if provided), and any information you share in the booking form or in the recorded discovery conversation.

(b) Payment information. Payments are processed by Stripe, Inc. We do not store, see, or transmit your card details. Stripe handles all payment data under its own privacy policy (stripe.com/privacy).

(c) Booking information. Booking scheduling is handled by Cal.com. Cal.com collects your name, email, time-zone, and any form responses you provide. Cal.com processes this data under its own privacy policy (cal.com/privacy).

(d) Usage information. The Site does not currently run any third-party analytics or behavioral tracking. Our hosting provider (Cloudflare) automatically logs standard technical data — such as IP address, browser type, and pages requested — for security, performance, and reliability. We do not use cross-site or cross-context tracking, and we do not build advertising profiles.

(e) Cookies and third-party fonts. The Site uses only essential cookies required for it to function and does not set advertising or analytics cookies. The Site loads typefaces from Google Fonts; when it does, your browser connects to Google's servers, which may receive your IP address. No other non-essential cookies are set.

2HOW WE USE INFORMATION

We use information to:

  • Deliver the Blueprint service you booked
  • Process payments
  • Send booking confirmations and follow-up correspondence
  • Improve the Site and our services
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

3THIRD PARTIES

We use the following third-party processors. Each is bound by its own privacy policy and applicable data-processing agreements:

  • Cal.com (booking and scheduling)
  • Stripe (payment processing)
  • Cloudflare (website hosting and delivery)
  • Google Fonts (typeface delivery)

We do not share your information with any third party for marketing purposes.

4DATA RETENTION

We retain personal information only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy or as required by law. Specifically:

  • Booking and discovery data: retained for the duration of our engagement plus 7 years (US tax and contract record-keeping requirements).
  • Payment records: retained as required by Stripe and US tax law (typically 7 years).
  • Analytics data: retained no longer than 24 months.

You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 6).

5YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your location, you have the following rights:

(a) For all visitors:

  • Access: request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: request that we correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: request that we delete your information, subject to legal retention requirements.

(b) For EU and UK residents (GDPR / UK GDPR):

  • Right to data portability
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

(c) For California residents (CCPA / CPRA):

  • Right to know what categories of personal information we collect and how we use them
  • Right to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information (we do not sell or share information for cross-context behavioral advertising)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

6HOW TO EXERCISE YOUR RIGHTS

Email ryan@blackrainautomations.com with the subject line "Privacy Request" and a description of your request. We will respond within 30 days (or sooner where required by law).

7INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

BlackRain Automations LLC is located in the United States. If you access the Site from outside the US, your information will be transferred to, processed, and stored in the United States. By using the Site, you consent to this transfer. For EU and UK visitors, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses where applicable, executed through our processors' data-processing agreements.

8CHILDREN

The Site is not directed to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

9SECURITY

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.
